DRUNKEN NOODLES

In case you hadn’t noticed, thai food is one of my favourite cuisines.

These noodles are spicy, salty, fragrant, and a little bit sweet.

Perfect whether you’re hungover or not!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 250g chicken cut into bite sized pieces
  • 200g broccoli
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp freshly grated ginger
  • 5 birds eye chillies
  • 225g/8oz instant rice noodles
  • 20g/1 cup loosely packed thai basil
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 2 green onions

    • SAUCE

  • 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp regular soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1-2 tsp sriracha

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare noodles per packet directions.
  2. Mix Sauce in a small bowl.
  3. Heat oil in wok or large heavy based skillet over high heat.
  4. Add garlic, ginger and chilli and cook for 10 seconds. Don't inhale - the chilli will make you cough!
  5. Add onion, cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add chicken and fish sauce, and fry until cooked, around 2 minutes.
  7. Add green onion, noodles and sauce and cook for 1 minute until the sauce reduces and coats the noodles.
  8. Remove from heat and immediately add basil, toss until just wilted, then serve immediately.
